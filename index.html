I Color

Color really is not that difficult to get right, If you know where to start.
Learn by doing and you will be a master in no time.






Blue

Blue is the color of sky and sea. It is often associated with Depth and Stability.It symbolises trust, loyalty, wisdom, confidence, intelligence, faith, truth and heaven.






Green

Green is the color of nature.It symbolises growth, harmony, freshness and fertility.Green has strong emotional correspondance with safety.






Red

Red is the color of fire and blood, so it is asssociated with energy, war, danger, strength, power, determination as well as passion, desire and love.
